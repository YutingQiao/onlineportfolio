About Me

Photography is not an interest to me, it came to be a hobbie. A way to encode memory - the texture of fluffy fur, the warmth of the skin of another body, the boiling of tea. The mood expressed by the photo existed in my life. All of it. Documenting is the foundation of creating. A camera is a tool to create illusions and stories based on my vision. As well as working with digital tools, I love to play with film and polaroid as well. I also do video works.

Several projects I have done are shown on this website I have created.

  • Anaglyph 3D Documentary (a pair of red and blue glasses may be needed to view the full effect)
  • Narrative - A project about a hot water bottle.
  • #Death - A documentary project about the road kill I have seen, dead animals on the street.
  • #Couple - Documenting couples on the street.
  • Travel - What’s behind the desert? An oasis or another desert?
  • Film photography - The effort of producing this shows my passion for photography.
  • Food - Who is in whose plate?
  • Pet - We work hard so they don’t.
  • Portrait - People in my life, they have different faces.
  • Experimental -What I am going to create is based on what I have already done.
